Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before beginning.

Thank you for participating in our study! In this study, we will ask you to watch a video and then describe what you see.

Each of the videos that you will see will have two actors. Your job is to write a single sentence giving a description of the video.

There are just two requirements:
  1. Your writing should be a good description of the video.
  2. You should write only ONE English sentence that is well-formed.
    One sentence means to use only one period in your writing.

    Examples of such one-sentence descriptions are:
  • The mother gave her boy some milk.
  • A girl and a boy had dinner together.
  • Some monkeys like to fight each other, but others don't.
  • The doctor entered the room, but did not speak.
  • Good students take great interest in their studies.

The videos are a few seconds long each, and will not have sound. When you are finished reading these instructions, click the button below to continue.
